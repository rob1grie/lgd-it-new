About Us

LGD Services was originally formed to provide IT and website services to churches and non-profits, but after visiting with church offices in the area I learned that most - if not all - had someone in their congregation already serving in that capacity. Then it dawned on me ... why should I be trying to "make a buck" off of God's Church?

That's when I changed my focus to supporting small businesses and homes, offering my services and experience to those that preferred not to pay "Geek Squad" prices. My conscientious work ethic, personable attitude, and affordable pricing were all it took for my customers to then let me prove my skills and knowledge in solving their IT and website needs.
